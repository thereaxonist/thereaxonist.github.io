About Me

I'm The Reaxonist, and I've waited thirty years to be here. Where is here? The intersection of mental alignment and freedom from the constraints of a standard American workday. I mashed my name up from different playdough colors - axons are the neurons responsible for thought, and the purpose of this site is to share how I react to and process anything and everything I learn. The -ist makes me sound professional! Thanks for stopping by, and I hope you stick around!
